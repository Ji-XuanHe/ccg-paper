# Rebuttal Tone

- **Respectful but firm**: "We respectfully disagree because..." NOT "The reviewer is wrong."
- **Concede small points**: Build trust by agreeing on 1-2 minor issues.
- **Evidence-first**: Lead with numbers, not arguments.
- **Avoid**: "Obviously", "As clearly stated", sarcasm, passive-aggressive.
- **Never**: Attack reviewer competence; speculate about their identity.
