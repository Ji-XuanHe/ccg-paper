# Rebuttal Salvage Strategies

- **Missing baseline**: Run it NOW (you have a week). Report in rebuttal.
- **Unclear claim**: Concede + reword for camera-ready.
- **Reviewer misread**: Quote the exact line they missed. Stay polite.
- **Limited novelty (hardest)**: Position against closest prior work with a table. Highlight new axes.
- **Weak experiments**: Add 1-2 decisive ones. Don't try to add 5 — reviewers distrust flood.
- **Theoretical gap**: Give intuition + commit to full proof in camera-ready.

Rule: **new experiments > arguments > promises**.
