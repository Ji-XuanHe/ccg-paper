# Connectors & Transitions

- **Contrast**: However, In contrast, Nevertheless, Whereas
- **Addition**: Moreover, Furthermore, Additionally, In addition
- **Causation**: Thus, Therefore, Consequently, As a result, Hence
- **Example**: For instance, Specifically, In particular, Notably
- **Concession**: Although, While, Despite, Even though

Avoid starting 3+ consecutive paragraphs with the same connector. Vary.
