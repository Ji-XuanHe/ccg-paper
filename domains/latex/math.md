# LaTeX Math

- **Operator names**: `\log, \sin, \exp` NOT `log, sin, exp` (upright, spacing correct).
- **Custom operators**: `\DeclareMathOperator{\softmax}{softmax}`
- **Bold vectors**: `\mathbf{x}` or `\bm{x}` (bm package, italic bold).
- **Expectation/Probability**: `\mathbb{E}, \mathbb{P}` (amssymb).
- **Sets**: `\mathcal{D}` for datasets, `\mathbb{R}` for reals.
- **Equation numbering**: Only number equations you reference. Use `\nonumber` otherwise.
- **Align**: `&=` aligns on equals sign; one `&` per line.
