# CVPR / ICCV / ECCV — Reviewer Preferences

- **Format**: 8 pages main + refs (CVPR: unlimited refs). Double blind.
- **Supplementary**: Video / extra qualitative results expected; often decisive.
- **Scoring**: Reviewers are harsh; average needed ~borderline to accept.
- **Rebuttal**: 1 page PDF, hard limit. Must address concerns with new experiments allowed.
- **Style**: Visual-first. Teaser figure on page 1 is standard.
- **Preference**: SOTA numbers + ablations + qualitative comparisons.

## Common rejects
- No qualitative figures
- Missing comparison to a recent arXiv paper (rebuttal-savable if pre-print date checks out)
- No failure cases discussed
