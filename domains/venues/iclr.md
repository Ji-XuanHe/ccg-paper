# ICLR — Reviewer Preferences

- **Format**: 9 pages (sometimes 10) + unlimited appendix. Double blind. OpenReview public discussion.
- **Distinct feature**: Open review — everyone sees reviews and rebuttal exchanges.
- **Tone**: Community-facing; reviewers may ping each other.
- **Preference**: Representation learning, ML foundations, creative ideas welcome.
- **Strong signals**: Ablations that isolate the claimed mechanism; clean figures; open code.
- **Rebuttal**: Multi-round — you can keep replying until deadline.

## OpenReview-specific tips
- Address each reviewer in separate comment, not one monolithic block
- "We thank reviewer" is fine; avoid groveling
- Post new experimental results as top-level comment (reviewers get notified)
