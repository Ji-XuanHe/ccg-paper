# KDD

- **Research Track**: 9 pages + refs. Double blind.
- **Preference**: Real-world datasets, deployed systems, case studies valued.
- **ADS track**: Applied Data Science — system / deployment focus.
- **Reproducibility**: Data + code strongly expected.

## Watch out
- Pure theory / pure ML papers often misfit — submit to ICML instead
- Scale & deployment evidence matter more than at ML-only venues
