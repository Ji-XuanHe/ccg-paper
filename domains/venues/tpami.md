# TPAMI / TMLR (Journal)

- **Format**: Unlimited pages; depth expected.
- **TPAMI**: Multi-round review, 3-12 months turn-around.
- **TMLR**: Claims-based review (not novelty-based); "are claims supported?" is the only bar.
- **Style**: Comprehensive related work, extended experiments, theoretical depth.

## Conference-to-journal extension
- At least 30% new content over conference version
- Disclose prior publication clearly
- Extend experiments / theory / related work
