# AAAI

- **Format**: 7 pages + 2 pages refs + appendix. Double blind.
- **Two-phase**: Phase 1 (triage) + Phase 2 (full review). Reject at Phase 1 kills paper.
- **Preference**: Broad AI; combinatorial / symbolic / applied AI more welcome than NeurIPS.
- **Style**: Shorter, more concise than NeurIPS/ICML.
